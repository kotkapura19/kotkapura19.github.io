Japji Sahib Da Arth – Pauri 1 Di Vyakhya

Japji Sahib Guru Nanak Dev Ji di bani hai. Eh Sri Guru Granth Sahib Ji da aarambh hai te Sikh Dharam da mool adhaar hai. Har Sikh di nitnem vich Japji Sahib shamil hai.

Mool Pankti

Sochai soch na hova-ee je sochee lakh vaar. Chupai chup na hova-ee je laa-e rahaa liv taar. Bhukhi-aa bhukh na utree je bannaa puree-aa bhaar. Sahas si-aanpaa lakh hohi ta ik na chalai naal. Kiv sachiaaraa ho-ee-ai kiv koorai tutai paal. Hukam rajaa-ee chalnaa Nanak likhiaa naal.

Line By Line Arth

Sochai soch na hova-ee je sochee lakh vaar

Sirf soch ke ya baharli safai karke mann pavittar nahi hunda, chahe lakh vaar koshish kariye.

Chupai chup na hova-ee

Chup reh ke ya moun dharan karke sach di prapti nahi hundi.

Bhukhi-aa bhukh na utree

Duniya di ichha kabhi khatam nahi hundi, chahe kitna v dhan jama kariye.

Sahas si-aanpaa lakh hohi

Lakh chaturaiyan v sadi madad nahi kar sakdiyan.

Kiv sachiaaraa ho-ee-ai?

Fir insaan sachha kiven ban sakda hai?

Hukam rajaa-ee chalnaa

Guru Nanak Dev Ji dasde ne — Rab de Hukam vich chalna hi sach di raah hai.

Japji Sahib Di Mahatva

Japji Sahib sade mann nu shant karda hai. Eh sanu ahankar ton door karda hai te Rab de hukam vich jeena sikhaunda hai.

Conclusion

Je asi roz Japji Sahib nu samajh ke padhiye, sada jeevan badal sakda hai. Waheguru de hukam vich chalna hi asli safalta hai.
