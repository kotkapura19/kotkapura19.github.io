Nitnem Banian Da Mahatva

Nitnem Sikh Dharam da rozana paath hai. Har Sikh nu subah-shaam bani da paath karna chahida hai. Nitnem sanu Rab naal jodda hai te mann vich shanti paida karda hai.

1️⃣ Japji Sahib

Japji Sahib Guru Nanak Dev Ji di bani hai. Eh sanu Rab de hukam, sach te jeevan di asli raah sikhaundi hai. Roz subah Japji Sahib da paath mann nu pavittar karda hai.

2️⃣ Jaap Sahib

Jaap Sahib Guru Gobind Singh Ji di bani hai. Is vich Waheguru di beant mahima bayan kiti gayi hai. Eh bani sanu Rab di vaddi taakat da ehsaas karaundi hai.

3️⃣ Rehras Sahib

Rehras Sahib shaam nu padhi jandi hai. Eh din bhar di thakan door karke mann vich himmat te shakti paida kardi hai.

4️⃣ Kirtan Sohila

Kirtan Sohila raat nu sone ton pehla padhi jandi hai. Eh bani sanu shanti te surakhya di bhavna dindi hai.

5️⃣ Anand Sahib

Anand Sahib khushi te anand di bani hai. Is da paath karan naal andar di udaasi door ho sakdi hai te jeevan vich sakoon mil sakda hai.

Nitnem Karna Kyon Zaroori Hai?

  • Mann di shanti layi
  • Ahankar ton door rehna
  • Rab de hukam vich jeena
  • Positive soch paida karna

Adhyatmik Laabh

Jo vyakti roz Nitnem karda hai, oh dheere-dheere apne jeevan vich badlav mehsoos karda hai. Soch pavittar hundi hai te mann vich dar ya ghabrahat ghat ho jandi hai.

Conclusion

Nitnem sirf paath nahi, par jeevan jeen di kala hai. Je asi roz imandari naal bani padhiye te samajh ke jeevan vich lagu kariye, sadi zindagi roshan ho sakdi hai.

Hor padhho: Japji Sahib Da Arth
