Japji Sahib Da Arth – Pauri 2 Te 3

Japji Sahib diyan har pauriyan sanu Rab di mahima te hukam di samajh dindiyaan han. Hun asi Pauri 2 te 3 di saral vyakhya samajhde haan.

Pauri 2

Hukmee hovan aakaar hukam na kahi-aa jaa-ee. Hukmee hovan jee-a hukam milai vadi-aa-ee. Hukmee uttam neech hukam likh dukh sukh paa-ee-ah. Iknaa hukmee bakhsees ik hukmee sadaa bhavaa-ee-ah. Hukmai andar sabh ko baahar hukam na ko-e. Nanak hukmai je bujhai ta haumai kahai na ko-e.

Arth

Is pauri vich Guru Nanak Dev Ji dasde ne ke sab kuch Rab de hukam naal hunda hai. Srishti di rachna, jeevan, sukh-dukh — sab hukam andar hai.

Je asi hukam nu samajh laiye ta sadi haumai (ahankar) khatam ho sakdi hai.

Pauri 3

Gaavai ko taan hovai kisai taan. Gaavai ko daat jaanai neesaan. Gaavai ko gun vadi-aa-ee-aa chaar. Gaavai ko vidiaa vikham veechar. Gaavai ko saaj kare tan kheh. Gaavai ko jeea lai fir deh. Gaavai ko jaapai disai door. Gaavai ko vekhai haadraa hadoor.

Arth

Koi Rab di taakat di tareef karda hai, koi usdi bakhshish di. Koi usde gun gaanda hai, koi usdi rachna di.

Kuj lok Rab nu door samajhde ne, par kuj usnu har jagah mehsoos karde ne.

Adhyatmik Sandesh

Eh pauriyan sanu sikhaundiyan han ke Rab har jagah maujood hai te sab kuch usde hukam vich ho reha hai. Ahankar chhad ke je asi hukam vich jeena sikh laiye, mann vich shanti aa sakdi hai.

Conclusion

Japji Sahib sanu sach di raah dikhaunda hai. Har pauri sade andar di soch nu badaldi hai. Roz Japji Sahib da paath te arth samajhna sade jeevan nu roshan kar sakda hai.
