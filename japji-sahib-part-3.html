Japji Sahib Da Arth – Pauri 4 Te 5

Japji Sahib di har pauri sanu Rab di bhagti te sach di samajh dindi hai. Hun asi Pauri 4 te 5 di saral vyakhya samajhde haan.

Pauri 4

Saacha sahib saach naa-e bhaakhiaa bhaao apaar. Aakhahi mangahi deh deh daat kare daataar. Pher ke agai rakhiai jit disai darbaar. Muhou ke bolan bolee-ai jit sun dhare piaar. Amrit velaa sach naa-o vadi-aa-ee veechar.

Arth

Rab sada sach hai te usda naam v sach hai. Asi usnu pyar naal yaad kariye. Oh sanu hamesha bakhshish dinda hai.

Guru Nanak Dev Ji dasde ne ke Amrit Vela vich Rab da naam japna sab ton uttam hai.

Pauri 5

Thaapiaa na jaa-e keeta na ho-e. Aape aap niranjan so-e. Jin sev-i-aa tin paa-i-aa maan. Nanak gaavee-ai gunee nidhaan.

Arth

Rab nu koi bana nahi sakda, oh apne aap ton hai. Jo usdi seva karde ne, oh izzat paande ne.

Guru Nanak Dev Ji kehnde ne ke Rab gunan da khazana hai, asi usdi mahima gauni chahidi hai.

Adhyatmik Sandesh

Eh pauriyan sanu sikhaundiyan han ke Rab sada sach hai. Naam japna, seva karni te nimrata naal jeena hi asli jeevan hai.

Conclusion

Japji Sahib sadi soch nu pavittar karda hai. Je asi Amrit Vela vich uth ke naam japie te arth samajh ke padhiye, sadi zindagi vich badlav aa sakda hai.
